Owned by the Community

Many existing social platforms are owned by tech giants.

Habitat is owned by everybody.

  • Free.
  • Open source.
  • Self-hosted.

Create a Habitat for your local area.

Invite people to join your Habitat and talk all things local.

  • No ads.
  • No data harvesting.
  • Just local conversation.

Location at the Heart

Users can discuss specific locations within their Habitat.

They can find out about conversations happening nearby.
